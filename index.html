From the vibrant cultures of Portugal and Angola, Francisco Catarro is making waves in the music world. Now a rising star in the UK, Francisco has shared the stage with up-and-coming talents like Story and Carla Prata, infusing their performances with his unique blend of jazz and neo-soul. His journey has seen him opening for legends such as Tom Jones and Lionel Richie, captivating audiences with every strum. With a deep passion for music that transcends borders, Francisco Catarro is a name to watch in the contemporary music scene.
